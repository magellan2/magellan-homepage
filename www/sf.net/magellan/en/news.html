2002.02.24 - New feature

The faction stats now also shows information about the production in the last week.

2002.02.14 - Problems with faction stats or skillcharts(chart.jar)

Due to some internal code modifications the latest Magellan versions are incompatible to the older versions of chart.jar. This may lead to problems with the faction stats, which might not work correct. If you're facing such problems either delete chart.jar from your resource paths or download the new version of chart.jar.

2001.12.29 - English Translation

The Magellan homepage is from now on also available in an English version.
